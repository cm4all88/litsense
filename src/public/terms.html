Platform Fee

LitSense charges a 10% platform fee on the sale price of each completed transaction, deducted from the seller's payout. Postage costs are separate and not subject to the platform fee.

Escrow & Payment Release

Buyer funds are authorized at the time of purchase but held (not captured) until the buyer confirms receipt of the item. If a buyer does not confirm or dispute within 14 days of the listed ship date, funds will be automatically released to the seller. This escrow process is facilitated via Stripe.
