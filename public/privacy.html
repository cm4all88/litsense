1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account information: Name, email address, and password (managed via Supabase authentication)
  • Reading preferences: Genres, moods, favorite books, and reading history you provide during onboarding or while using the Service
  • Shelf data: Books you add to your reading lists, shelves, and collections
  • User content: Reviews, ratings, community thread posts, and other content you submit
  • Marketplace data: Book listings you create, transaction history, and shipping information for book swaps
  • Payment information: Billing details are collected and stored by Stripe. We receive only limited payment confirmation data (last 4 digits, payment status) — never full card numbers.
  • Communications: Messages you send to our support team

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage data: Pages visited, features used, search queries, interaction patterns, session duration
  • Device information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, device type
  • Log data: IP address, access times, referring URLs, error logs
  • Interaction data: Books you save, dismiss, view, or click; recommendation engagement

Information from Device Features (When You Consent)

  • Camera: Images captured during bookshelf scanning, processed to identify book titles
  • Microphone: Voice input during voice-based shelf intake, processed to recognize spoken text

4. AI & Data Processing

LitSense uses AI models (provided by Anthropic) to power Sage and our recommendation engine. When you interact with Sage or request recommendations:

  • Your messages and reading context are sent to Anthropic's API for processing.
  • Anthropic processes this data subject to their own privacy policy and data use agreements.
  • LitSense does not use your personal conversations with Sage to train AI models without your explicit consent.
  • Aggregated and anonymized patterns from how users interact with recommendations may be used to improve LitSense's own recommendation logic.

You may opt out of having your interaction data used for recommendation improvement by contacting us at privacy@litsense.app.

5. Camera & Microphone Data

Camera (Bookshelf Scanning): When you use the bookshelf scanning feature, your device camera captures images that are sent to our AI processing pipeline (via Anthropic's Vision API) to identify book titles and covers. These images are processed in real-time and are not stored on LitSense servers after processing is complete. Identified book data is saved to your shelf only if you confirm the results.

Microphone (Voice Intake): When you use voice-based shelf intake, audio is captured locally by your browser using the Web Speech API. Speech recognition is processed on your device or through your browser's built-in speech services. LitSense does not receive or store raw audio recordings. Only the recognized text output is sent to LitSense's servers.

Both features require your explicit browser permission and can be disabled at any time through your browser or device settings.

6. Cookies & Local Storage

LitSense uses browser storage technologies including cookies and localStorage for the following purposes:

  • Essential: Authentication session tokens (managed by Supabase), security tokens. These are required for the Service to function.
  • Functional: Your app preferences, onboarding completion status, welcome screen display state, usage counter for rate limiting, and UI state.
  • Analytics: Aggregated, non-personally-identifying usage patterns to help us understand how the Service is used.

LitSense does not use third-party advertising cookies or sell cookie data to advertisers. You can clear localStorage data through your browser settings, which will reset certain app preferences. See our Cookie Notice for full details.

7. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with the Service. Specifically:

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account, then deleted within 30 days.
  • Reading preferences & shelf data: Retained with your account. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years as required by tax and financial regulations.
  • Marketplace transaction records: Retained for 7 years for legal and dispute purposes.
  • AI interaction logs: Retained for up to 90 days, then deleted.
  • Camera/voice processing data: Not retained beyond the active session (see Section 5).

8. Your Rights & Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data. You can delete your account from account settings, which initiates the deletion process. Note that some data may be retained for legal obligations.
  • Portability: Request your reading shelf and preference data in a portable format.
  • Opt-out of marketing emails: Unsubscribe via the link in any marketing email or by contacting us.
  • Withdraw consent: Where we rely on consent to process your data, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@litsense.app. We will respond within 30 days.

9. California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) and California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA) give you specific rights in addition to those described above:

  • Right to Know: You can request disclosure of the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you, the purposes for collection, and the categories of third parties with whom we share it.
  • Right to Delete: You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale/Sharing: LitSense does not sell or share your personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: We will not discriminate against you for exercising your privacy rights.
  • Right to Correct: You can request correction of inaccurate personal information.
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Information: We do not use sensitive personal information beyond what is necessary to provide the Service.

To submit a CCPA request, email privacy@litsense.app with the subject line "California Privacy Request." We will verify your identity before processing requests.

Shine the Light: California Civil Code Section 1798.83 also permits you to request information regarding disclosure of personal information to third parties for direct marketing. LitSense does not share personal information with third parties for their own direct marketing purposes.

10. Children's Privacy

LitSense is not directed to children under 13 years of age, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13 without verifiable parental consent, we will take steps to delete such information as soon as possible. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us at privacy@litsense.app.

Users between 13 and 17 years of age may use LitSense with the consent of their parent or legal guardian. Parents who believe their minor child has submitted personal information without consent may contact us to request deletion.

11. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encrypted data transmission via HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication tokens managed by Supabase with industry-standard security practices
  • Payment data isolation through Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure
  • Access controls limiting employee access to personal data
  • Regular security reviews of our application and infrastructure

No method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that affects your rights and freedoms, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

12. International Users

LitSense is operated from the United States. If you access the Service from outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States, where data protection laws may differ from those in your country. By using LitSense, you consent to this transfer.

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we rely on the following legal bases for processing: contractual necessity, legitimate interests, and your consent where required. You have additional rights under GDPR including the right to lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.
